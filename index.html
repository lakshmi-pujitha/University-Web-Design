India's Best University

Sundaram, the premier promoter of quality education in the coastal districts of Tamilnadu for the past two decades, leads various institutions ranging from K.G to P.G besides professional colleges like Engineering, Pharmacy and Nursing. Sri Sundaram Eswaran as a founder chairman, promoted the educational society in the name and style of Sundaram University at Tamilnadu in the year 1984, with a vision and mission to create a platform for holistic growth and success to students at all levels.
